auto_stories The Story

EasyNote is a fast-moving startup that recently deployed its internal note-taking application. In their rush to launch, the development team prioritized functionality over security. You have been contracted to perform a black-box penetration test against their infrastructure. Your objective is simple: breach their external perimeter, exploit their custom web application, and escalate privileges to achieve total system compromise. Keep an eye out for how they handle user inputs, file uploads, and routine background tasks.
